Bio

After obtaining an undergraduate degree in Psychology from King’s College London, I am currently a master’s student in Neural and Behavioural Sciences at the Graduate Training Center of Neuroscience in Tuebingen. In this vein, I am thrilled to join the Human and Machine Cognition Lab for my MSc thesis on structural learning! I am specifically interested in strategies used by biological and artificial agents to extract meaningful information from data, how they generalise information to new contexts and how they decide which learning strategy to choose.
