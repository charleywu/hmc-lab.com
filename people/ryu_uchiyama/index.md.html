Bio

Ryu is a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Tübingen and the Tübingen AI Center, working with Charley Wu and Claudio Tennie. His current research focuses on understanding the structure of cultural learning and cumulative culture through the lens of cognitive computation. Ryu was previously Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore, and has obtained a PhD from the London School of Economics as well as a MA from Cornell University and a BSc from the University of Lethbridge, Canada. His research background spans cognitive science, neuroscience, evolutionary biology, and cultural evolution.
