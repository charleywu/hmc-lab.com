Bio

I completed my B.Sc. in Business Informatics at Heilbronn University and investigated in my bachelor thesis how implicit motives influence group work. During and after my studies I worked as a software developer in web development and machine learning. Currently, I study Cognitive Science in the Master’s program at the University of Tübingen and am interested in interdisciplinary research domains between machine learning and human cognition.
