Postdoc position -- Social Learning and Cultural Evolution
posted on November 7, 2025

We are currently seeking a highly motivated individual to fill a three-year postdoc position (100% TV-E14) working on topics related to social learning and cultural evolution. You will have substantial freedom to develop and pursue your own research ideas and benefit from a vibrant interdisciplinary environment and strong collaborative networks. The position is designed to be co-advised by Prof. Dr. Dominik Deffner at the University of Marburg.

The position is designed to enable you to further expand your expertise and advance your academic career, with ample opportunity to establish your own lines of inquiry. Your role include providing guidance and scientific support to PhD researchers and other students in the group.

You should have (or be expecting to have) a PhD degree in a related discipline before starting this position and prior experience with computational methods is highly desirable. The start date is open to negotiation. Applications are due by December 4th, 2025 or until filled

Please see the official call for applications below or download the PDF for more details.

Submit your application here: https://www.career.tu-darmstadt.de/#/tu-darmstadt/apply/51386.
